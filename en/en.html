About

TTropic Groupe was founded by Leo (alias F_Gammer) in 2020. Tropic Groupe is just an organization, we do not have any employees, our staff members are only volunteers and we thank them for working with us. We are therefore a non-profit organization. Today our team is composed of 3 members.

Our history

Tropic Groupe appeared thanks to discord. At the time Leo had created during the pandemic a discord server to bring together many people to discuss and help each other. A few months later a Minecraft server was created as well as a website to support it was the birth of Tropic Groupe. Tropic group grew with the creation of many websites such as Tropic News (no longer exists) or Tropic Games.
